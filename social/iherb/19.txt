Featuring the organic coconut butter from @kevala_organics coconut butter and pineapple, this refreshing pina colada smoothie is your passport to the tropics without even leaving your kitchen. Recipe by @kristinashyped

Pina Colada Protein Smoothie

1 cup frozen pineapple
3 Tbs. @kevala_organics coconut butter
1 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 scoop vanilla protein powder 
1/2 frozen banana
.
Add all the ingredients to your blender. Blend it all up in a high-speed blender and you instantly have a vacation in a glass.
